# Sudden Death

![demo GIF](demo.gif)

basically just the fornite storm

- everyone's config must match or your visuals will not match the host, who is the one that sends out the damage
- regular matchmaking disabled for that reason

## Default Config Values
- Death Zone Color: 110, 53, 45
- Death Zone Starting Radius: 37.5 units
- Death Zone Minimum Radius: 10 units
- Death Zone Shrink Rate: 1 unit / sec
- Seconds until Zone Appears: 45 sec
- DPS of Zone: 10 DPS
